# Claim Boundary

This repository supports a finite `S4` paper with the following claims:

- one-line `S4` words, local prefix flags, and permutation-matrix supports are canonically bijective as indexing models;
- the selected data package records 226 selected artifact rows, 219 strict labeled posets giving 219 distinct poset-cone subsets, 221 exact poset-cone artifact rows, and 30 subgroups under the stated convention;
- common-precedence closure gives an exact finite criterion for nonempty subsets that are linear-extension sets of recovered posets;
- the fixed `D4` and `V4` rows are subgroup rows but not exact poset cones;
- the fixed `D4` and `V4` subgroup rows have the corresponding left and right coset partitions by finite group theory;
- the N-poset witness is an exact poset cone but not a subgroup row;
- selected witnesses show exact poset-cone status is not equivalent to subgroup-row status.

This package does not claim:

- classification of all subsets of `S4`;
- classification of all exact covers or tilers;
- a geometric realization theorem;
- a general `S_n` theorem;
- an independent theorem separating subgroup-row status from subgroup-coset partition status;
- that the selected artifact-row counts are a full atlas of all `S4` subsets.
